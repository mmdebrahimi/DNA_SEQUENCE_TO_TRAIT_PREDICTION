## What's deployed (v0.3.2 — the milestone)

`dna-decode` unified CLI dispatches to two deterministic decoders:

| Decoder | What | Validation |
|---|---|---|
| `dna-amr` | E. coli R/S for cipro / cef / tet / gent, from AMRFinderPlus curated determinants | in-cohort 0.94 · held-out 0.86 · **cross-source (NCBI, N=22, zero BV-BRC overlap) 0.86–1.0** |
| `dna-pathotype` | E. coli pathotype compatibility (EPEC/EHEC/ETEC/UPEC/EAEC) + abstention | VF-marker resolver; ExPEC recall 0.917 |

Key engineering wins this session:
- **Per-drug `DRUG_RULE`** (`dna_decode/eval/amr_rules.py`): cipro threshold=2 (QRDR); cef/gent threshold=1
  **+ AMRFinder-Subclass refinement** (CEPHALOSPORIN/CARBAPENEM for cef; GENTAMICIN for gent). The naive
  "any drug-class determinant→R" over-calls (cef spec 0.41, gent 0.39); the Subclass refinement is the
  value-add (cef→0.933, gent→0.945).
- **Cross-source validation PROVES the layer adds value** over vanilla AMRFinder on un-tuned data:
  +0.09 (cipro) / +0.23 (cef) / +0.41 (gent) accuracy. `wiki/dna_amr_xsource_validation_2026-06-07.md`.
- **Honesty taxonomy:** S calls carry `undetectable_mechanisms` (efflux/porin_loss/regulatory blind spots);
  `evaluate_cohort` emits FN/FP discordance buckets; provenance pins the AMRFinder image.

## The strategic arc (why we are where we are)

The embedding thesis (NT frozen mean-pool + XGBoost beats classical/knowledge baselines) was tested to a
**decisive 0-for-3 FAIL** on every reachable substrate:
1. **pathotype** — labels are sampling-confounded isolation-source categories (unvalidatable).
2. **AMR (cipro, cleanest de-confounded substrate)** — NT 0.914 LOST to QRDR-POINT 0.943; within-lineage
   concordance = chance ⇒ NT learned lineage, not mechanism.
3. **carbon-utilization** (the survey's top "open niche") — E. coli depth is 27 strains (data acquired,
   Li et al. OSF); 0 carbon sources clear the ≥100 floor → infeasible.

**Binding constraint (now proven 3×):** de-confounded, sampling-independent, organism-DEEP labeled
substrate. It doesn't exist at solo scale outside AMR. The embedding-niche test needs THREE things:
sampling-independent label + no curated catalog + organism depth at scale. ⇒ **Pivot: ship the
deterministic decoders. Done + deployed.**
