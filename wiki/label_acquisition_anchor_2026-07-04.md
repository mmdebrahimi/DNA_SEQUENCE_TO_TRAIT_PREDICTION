## The anchor — why acquisition is THE lever now (everything else is code-closed)

As of the 2026-07-04 terminal, the project has exhaustively established: **the binding constraint is LABELS,
not models.** Every learned-model expansion is closed with a recorded negative, and every FREE independent
label path has been either exploited (AMR Portal → 10 SCORED provdisjoint cells + TB independent number; HIV
PhenoSense; CoV-RDB) or proven exhausted (TB gold-set ×7 sources; SARS Mpro held-out empty). A non-public /
biobank measured-label source is the one lever that unlocks genuinely-new value, and it unlocks **TWO** things
at once:

1. **External validation of the DEPLOYED decoder** on independent measured labels — the human-trait cells
   (eye/ABO/lactase/earwax) are currently openSNP SELF-REPORT (PILOT/DEMO tier); a biobank's LAB-MEASURED
   phenotypes upgrade them to real validation, and give the bacterial/viral cells a fresh external cohort.

2. **The ONE UNRUN EXPERIMENT — the learned decoder's genuinely-fair test.** The embedding arm is 0-for-5,
   BUT every one of those negatives was on a substrate MISSING a leg of the embedding-niche 3-part test
   (sampling-independent MEASURED label + NO curated catalog + organism DEPTH ≥100). AMR fails "no catalog";
   pathotype fails "sampling-independent"; carbon-util fails "depth"; Arabidopsis flowering met all three but
   is a single quantitative trait. **A biobank (All of Us / UK Biobank) with a deep, lab-measured human trait
   that has no curated determinant catalog is the FIRST substrate that could satisfy all three at once** — the
   fair learned-decoder test that has never been possible. This is the real scientific prize.
