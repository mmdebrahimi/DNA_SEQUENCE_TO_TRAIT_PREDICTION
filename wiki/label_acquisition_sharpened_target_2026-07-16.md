## The sharpened target (the B-lens)

A label source is worth acquiring iff it clears BOTH gates the free space fails:
1. **Curated-causal-locus fit** — a curated catalog of *causal* loci exists (or can be built), so the
   deterministic decoder (the project's winning regime) applies. Polygenic/GWAS-score traits fail this
   (PGS-owned, ancestry-confounded) — do NOT acquire them.
2. **Independent, non-public, measured label** — the label is a *measurement* (MIC, assay, observed trait),
   NOT derived from the same tool the decoder would compete with, AND not already free.

Target in one line: **non-public MEASURED labels for a curated-causal-locus trait in a new organism/context.**
