## Honest gaps

- **Contrast with the parent topic:** vaccine seroprotection HAS established per-pathogen numeric correlates (this is the opposite of therapeutic-protein ADA, where no universal clinical threshold exists). But every correlate is pathogen- + assay-specific and mostly derived decades ago; several are population/setting-dependent (influenza 1:40 = ~50% in adults but only ~22% in children where ~1:110 is needed; household exposure lowers it).
- **No established correlate of protection** exists for several vaccines: pertussis, mumps, HPV (efficacy-based, no serologic threshold), RSV — omitted rather than fabricated.
- Rabies (0.5 IU/mL) + polio (1:8) not directly fetched (AUP filter / not fetched) → carried low; direct WHO-position-paper confirmation recommended.
- CDC HepB MMWR (rr6210a1) returned HTTP 403 on direct fetch → HepB row carried at medium via search-summary.
