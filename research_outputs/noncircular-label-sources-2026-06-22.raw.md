## Honest gaps

- **The survey RE-CONFIRMS the project wall ("labels not models").** For E. coli specifically, the free + measured + genome-linked + non-circular pool is essentially the open INSDC/BV-BRC set already exhausted; no new free E. coli measured-phenotype+genome source surfaced.
- **No DUA-gated bacterial AST treasure trove exists** — controlled access (EGA/dbGaP) is for human data; bacterial AST+genomes are open. So the "non-public clinical label" unlock is narrower than the anchor assumed.
- **ARESdb / 11,087-isolate access model unverified** — Ares Genetics is commercial; likely paid or MTA. Needs a primary-source fetch + a money/authority decision.
- **Whittam DECA, NARMS-genome-linkage, BacDive depth, SENTRY-genome-linkage** not deep-verified this pass (budget); SENTRY is bundled into the ATLAS site (same no-genome limitation); BacDive is type-strain-depth (fails organism-depth) by its known design.
- **Quantitative non-AMR assay phenotypes with single-organism depth** (growth/fitness/biochemical) — none surfaced at ≥100-isolate same-organism depth with public genomes; remains an open gap, not a found source.
